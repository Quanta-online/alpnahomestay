Exam Instructions

  1. Exam Duration: The total duration of the exam is 120 minutes (2 hours). The timer will begin when you start the exam and cannot be paused.
  2. Exam Structure: The test consists of 100 multiple-choice questions divided into 5 sections:
    • Tourist Management (20 questions)
    • Hospitality Management (20 questions)
    • Travel Management (20 questions)
    • Managerial Aptitude (20 questions)
    • General Knowledge (20 questions)
  3. Scoring System:
    • Each correct answer: +1 point
    • Each incorrect answer: -0.33 points (negative marking)
    • Unanswered questions: 0 points
  4. Exam Monitoring: Your exam session will be monitored through:
    • Live video feed via your webcam
    • Audio monitoring via your microphone
    • Location verification via GPS
    • Browser activity tracking
  5. Prohibited Activities: The following actions are strictly prohibited and may result in exam termination:
    • Switching browser tabs or opening new windows
    • Using any secondary devices (phones, tablets, etc.)
    • Communicating with others during the exam
    • Accessing study materials or external resources
    • Attempting to copy or screenshot exam questions
  6. Technical Requirements:
    • Stable internet connection (minimum 2Mbps recommended)
    • Modern browser (Chrome 90+, Firefox 85+, Edge 90+)
    • Webcam (720p resolution minimum)
    • Microphone (built-in or external)
    • Enable JavaScript and cookies
  7. Before Starting:
    • Ensure proper lighting in your room
    • Close all unnecessary applications
    • Keep your government-issued ID ready for verification
    • Use a quiet, private space with no interruptions
  8. During the Exam:
    • Keep your face visible to the camera at all times
    • Do not cover your mouth or look away frequently
    • Remain in the camera frame throughout the session
    • Do not use headphones unless explicitly permitted
  9. After Completion: Your results will be displayed immediately after submission. Official score reports will be emailed within 3 business days.
  10. Malpractice Consequences: Any violation of exam rules will result in automatic disqualification and may lead to permanent blacklisting from future Alpana Group recruitment processes.
